The Best Movies According to Renee

My top 3 movies of all time

Lion in Winter

I love learning about the lives of real people from throughout history. This was a well crafted and superbly acted movie depicting the lives of King Henry II of England and his wife Eleanor of Aquitaine.

Avalon

As someone who has done much research into genealogy and family history, this movie was such a wonderful example of how a family changes over a few generations from the time the first generation of the family comes to America

The Burbs

The bizarre happenings in this seemingly normal neighborhood remind me of the neighborhood in which I live. The movie is classified under the categories of Comedy and Horror. That is a pretty accurate description of my neighborhood!
